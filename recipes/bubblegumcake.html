Bubblegum Cake

Candy Cake? What?

Candy, flour, sugar, and icing OH MY!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of bubble gum
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup baking powder
  • 6 eggs
  • 9 TBSP EVOO
  • Can of icing
  • Blue and green food coloring
  • Candles

Instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 1,000 degrees F
  2. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl
  3. Combine wet ingredients in small bowl
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients and pour into the already-greased cake pan
  5. Bake for 3 hours
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 days
  7. Apply icing with wooden spoon while your dogs watch
  8. Add bubblegum pieces
  9. Eat cake
