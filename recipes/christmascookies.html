Christmas Cookies

Christmas Cookies for Christmas-Time

These adorable cookies will surely get your old grinch heart in the mood for this consumer-based holiday that we're all tricked into thinking matters way more than it does!

Ingredients

  • Sprinkles
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 TSP vanilla extract
  • 5 cups coconut shavings
  • 8 sticks of butter
  • Icing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 8,000 degrees F
  2. Combine all the ingredients
  3. Flatten the mixture on a parchment-paper covered cutting board
  4. Use your cookie cutters to make a bunch of candy canes, snowmen, and star shaped cookies
  5. Put those uncooked cookies on your foil-lined cookie sheet
  6. Bake for 33.5 minutes
  7. Let cool overnight
  8. Add icing and eat
